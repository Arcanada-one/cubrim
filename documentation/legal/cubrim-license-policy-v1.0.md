# Cubrim License Policy v1.0

This policy package is an engineering/legal-policy draft, not legal advice.
English is the primary drafting language. Russian text is a parallel
translation for operator and user comprehension.

## Model

Cubrim uses a dual-path model:

- Free non-commercial use under PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0, with
  Cubrim-specific notices in `code/cubrim-rs/LICENSE`.
- Paid commercial use under `code/cubrim-rs/LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.md` or another
  written agreement signed by Arcanada.

The temporary canonical publication target is `cubrim.com`. The intended
future canonical target is Legal Arcana at `legal.arcanada.ai`.

## Modal Summary: English

Cubrim is free for non-commercial use under PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0.
Commercial use requires a paid Arcanada commercial license.
Standard commercial target: USD 50/year per named user seat or per installed device.
Until Legal Arcana launches, canonical license text is published through cubrim.com.

## Modal Summary: Russian

Cubrim бесплатен для некоммерческого использования по PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0.
Коммерческое использование требует платной коммерческой лицензии Arcanada.
Стандартный ориентир: 50 долларов США в год за именованное место или установленное устройство.
До запуска Legal Arcana канонический текст лицензии публикуется через cubrim.com.

## Required Legal Review

Professional counsel should review this package before commercial enforcement
or sales launch. In particular, Arcanada should complete governing law, venue,
payment, tax, export-control, privacy, and support terms if the commercial
license becomes a production sales document.
