entity that accepts these terms or purchases a commercial Cubrim license

### 2. Licensed Software

"Software" means the Cubrim compression software, including source code,

### 3. Commercial Grant

limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable license to install,

commercial business purposes during the paid license term.

### 4. License Metric and Price

The standard commercial price for this version is USD 50 per year for each
licensed unit.

A "licensed unit" is either:

- one named user seat, meaning one identified individual authorized to use the
  Software on Customer's behalf; or
- one computer, server, virtual machine, container host, build worker, or other
  device on which the Software is installed or made available for execution.

Customer must use the metric stated in the purchase confirmation or signed
order. If no metric is stated, the device metric applies. Customer may not use a
single seat to cover shared, pooled, automated, or service-account use by
multiple people or devices.

### 5. Term and Renewal

Each commercial license runs for one year from the effective purchase date
unless a signed order states a different start date. Rights expire at the end
of the paid term unless renewed. Continued commercial use after expiration
requires renewal or a new written license from Licensor.

### 6. How to Purchase

Commercial licenses are purchased through Arcanada sales or the Arcanada
Support Center. Until a dedicated purchase URL is published, contact Arcanada
through the support channel listed on `cubrim.com`.

### 7. Restrictions

Customer must not:

- remove copyright, license, or required notices;
- sell, rent, lease, sublicense, or provide the Software as a standalone
  competing product;
- use the Software outside the number of paid licensed units;
- bypass technical restrictions or license controls, if any are provided in a
  future release;
- use Arcanada names, marks, or logos except as separately authorized in
  writing.

### 8. Ownership

Licensor and its licensors retain all ownership and intellectual property
rights in the Software. Customer receives only the rights expressly granted in
this license.

### 9. Feedback

If Customer gives suggestions, bug reports, benchmarks, or other feedback,
Customer permits Licensor to use that feedback without restriction or payment,
provided Licensor does not disclose Customer confidential information.

### 10. Warranty Disclaimer

The Software is provided as-is unless a signed order says otherwise. To the
maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Licensor disclaims implied
warranties, including merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose,
non-infringement, and title.

### 11. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Licensor will not be liable

consequential damages, special damages, incidental damages, or punitive
damages. Licensor's aggregate liability under this license will not exceed the
amount Customer paid for the affected licensed units during the twelve months

### 12. Termination

This license terminates automatically if Customer materially breaches it and
does not cure the breach within thirty days after written notice, or
immediately if the breach cannot reasonably be cured. After termination,
Customer must stop commercial use and delete or disable commercial deployments
unless a separate written agreement allows continued use.

### 13. Relationship to Non-Commercial License

This commercial license grants rights for commercial use. If Customer does not
hold an active commercial license, Customer may use Cubrim only to the extent
permitted by the applicable non-commercial license or another written agreement
from Licensor.

### 14. Legal Review Note

This draft intentionally avoids choosing governing law, venue, tax handling,
export control language, privacy terms, support service levels, and payment
processor terms. Those provisions should be completed by Arcanada with counsel
before production use.
